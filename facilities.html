    FACILITIES


    At Zamboanga del Norte National High School Turno, the faculties comprise a comprehensive array of resources designed to enhance student learning and development. From the cutting-edge EIM Lab, Chem Lab, and Bio Lab to creative spaces like the Rondalla Room and Instrumental Music Room, our facilities cater to diverse interests. Specialized areas such as the BPP Lab, Cookery Lab, and Technical Drafting Room, alongside essential resources like the Library Hub and ICT Lab, equip students for academic success and foster a culture of innovation and excellence.
